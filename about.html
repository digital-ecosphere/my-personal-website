About Me

Born in the Himalayas

I grew up amid the Himalayan forests, trained as a forester and remote‑sensing ecologist, and now apply AI‑driven geospatial tools to monitor and protect our planet.

Science & Spirituality

My approach blends rigorous science with spiritual reflection, guided by respect for creation and a commitment to sustainable stewardship.

Spiritual Inspiration

  • Surah Al‑Fatiha – Opening chapter; a daily call for mercy and guidance.
  • Shahada – Core declaration of faith.
  • Ayat‑ul‑Kursi – Verse of divine guardianship and protection.

Interfaith Reflection

For me, these sacred words are not just prayers but a bridge between traditions—reminders of divine love, wisdom, and peace. Whether in the Vedic hymns, the Psalms, the Buddha’s teachings, or the Quranic verses, I see a shared spiritual heritage calling us toward compassion, justice, and unity.
