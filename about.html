About Me

Born in the Himalayas

I grew up amid the Himalayan forests, trained as a forester and remote‑sensing ecologist, and now apply AI‑driven geospatial tools to monitor and protect our planet.

My approach weaves rigorous science with deep spiritual reflection, guided by respect for creation and a commitment to sustainable stewardship.

Science & Spirituality

I explore how data‑driven insight and faith can work together to promote environmental justice and human flourishing.

My Spiritual Inspiration

☪️ Islam

Three prayers that ground my daily practice:

  • Surah Al‑Fatiha (1:1–7) – The opening chapter of the Quran, invoking guidance and mercy.
  • Shahada – La ilaha illa Allah, Muhammadur Rasulullah – the declaration of faith.
  • Ayat‑ul‑Kursi (2:255) – A reminder of the Ever‑Living, Sustainer of all existence.

These verses inspire wisdom, justice, and compassion in my work and life.
