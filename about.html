I grew up in the embrace of Himalayan forests—
trained as a forester, evolving into a remote-sensing ecologist,
now weaving artificial intelligence with Earth observation
to serve our living planet.

Science meets Silence

My journey is not just data and algorithms.
It is also about silence, stillness, and reverence.
I believe sustainability begins with inner balance.
Scientific rigor guided by spiritual reflection.

Wisdom from Four Streams

I draw light from traditions I have lived and loved:

  • Hinduism: The Gita’s message of dharma and devotion grounds my ethics.
  • Buddhism: The mindfulness path deepens my awareness of change and compassion.
  • Christianity: Teachings of mercy and unconditional love inspire my service.
  • Islam: Quranic verses guide me with their majestic cadence of unity and mercy.

Bridges, Not Walls

To me, sacred texts are not claims to division but
shared invitations to truth.
The Psalms and the Dhammapada,
the Vedas and the Quran—
they sing in different tones, yet harmonize as one.

Harmonizing the Inner and Outer

My work as a scientist flows with spiritual insight.
My prayers are not separate from my pixels and data.
Through this blend of inquiry and awe,
I continue my journey—grateful, grounded, and whole.
