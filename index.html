About Me

Born in the Himalayas

I was naturally drawn to the intricate balance of nature and the wisdom it holds. Trained as a forester and ecologist, I specialize in remote sensing, utilizing advanced geospatial technologies to understand landscapes, environmental changes, and ecosystem dynamics. My expertise extends into artificial intelligence, where I integrate AI-driven insights to enhance environmental monitoring, conservation, and sustainable resource management.

Beyond my scientific pursuits, my journey is deeply rooted in spirituality. Coming from a Hindu family tradition, I have embraced the wisdom of multiple faiths—Christianity, Buddhism, and Islam—finding harmony in their shared values of compassion, mindfulness, service, and devotion to the Divine. To me, science and spirituality are not opposing forces but complementary perspectives that guide my work and my worldview. Whether studying the forests from above through remote sensing or seeking deeper truths through faith, I strive to bridge knowledge and wisdom for a more sustainable and meaningful future.

Science Meets Spirituality

Rooted in Hinduism as my family tradition, I have grown to embrace the wisdom and spiritual depth of Christianity, Buddhism, and Islam, seeing them not as contradictions but as different paths toward understanding the Divine. From Hinduism, I inherit a deep sense of devotion (Bhakti), the philosophy of unity (Advaita), and the sacred connection between the self and the universe. In Christianity, I find the call to love, grace, and selfless service, which align with the principles of dharma. In Buddhism, the practice of mindfulness and inner peace guides me toward self-discipline and compassion. In Islam, I embrace the profound surrender to the One God (Tawhid), the importance of justice, and the call to live a righteous life.

Rather than viewing these traditions as separate, I see them as different expressions of the same eternal truth—leading toward a life of faith, wisdom, and harmony. Across these traditions, I find inspiration in sacred words and prayers that uplift the soul. These powerful mantras, verses, and supplications remind me of the divine presence in every moment of life.
