International Journal of Digital Earth (IJDE) — https://www.tandfonline.com/journals/tjde20

International Journal of Digital Earth focuses on the theories, technologies, applications and societal implications of Digital Earth.

It is the official journal of the International Society for Digital Earth.

The journal responds to the Digital Earth initiative and its aim to improve social conditions, protect the environment, and support future sustainable development.
